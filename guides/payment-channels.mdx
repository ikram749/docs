---
title: "Payment Channels"
---

SayabeliPay supports multiple payment channels.

| Channel          | Description                                     |
| ---------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| `ONLINE_BANKING` | Pay through online banking within Malaysia      |
| `CC`             | Pay with debit or credit card                   |

👉 If `ONLINE_BANKING` is used, you must also specify a `payment_code `from the supported list.
